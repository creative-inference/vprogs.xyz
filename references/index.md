## [Sources & Links](/references/sources)

A categorized collection of all reference material used across this site. Organized by:

- **Primary research** -- proposals and formal models from the Kaspa Research Forum
- **Official Kaspa** -- kaspa.org publications and the KII Foundation
- **Community and articles** -- Medium posts, Reddit discussions, and media coverage
- **Technical concepts** -- key resources for each major technical area
- **Kaspa Research Forum** -- threads on rollup design, bridges, and sequencing
