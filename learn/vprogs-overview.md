# Welcome to Kaspa vProgs

Verifiable Programs (vProgs) are lightweight, deterministic logic modules native to Kaspa's Layer 1. They represent a revolutionary "third way" for blockchain programmability, sidestepping the bloat of traditional L1 smart contracts and the fragmentation of L2 rollups.

## The Vision

Kaspa is evolving into a universal programmable settlement layer. By combining **off-chain execution** with **on-chain ZK verification**, vProgs offer native synchronous composability. This ensures that decentralized applications can interact instantly without bridges or delays.

## Under the Hood

The foundation for vProgs is actively being laid in the core node software. The recent integration of the Rusty-Kaspa 2.0 (`rk2.0`) update into the main codebase marks a massive leap forward in performance and stability, providing the underlying infrastructure required to deploy L1 programmable applications on Kaspa securely.
