## How It All Connects

The ecosystem is structured around a layered dependency chain:

```
GHOSTDAG --> DagKnight (consensus ordering)
                |
          Covenants++ (L1 programmability primitives)
                |
          vProgs (off-chain execution + on-chain ZK verification)
                |
          Applications (DeFi, DAOs, enterprise, privacy)
```

Each layer enables the next. The [Roadmap](/ecosystem/roadmap) tracks progress across all four phases, while [Applications](/ecosystem/applications) details what becomes possible at each stage.

The [KII Foundation](/ecosystem/kii) works to bring enterprise and institutional demand to the platform, and the [Community](/ecosystem/community) provides the connective tissue between developers, researchers, and users.
